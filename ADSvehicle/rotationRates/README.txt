This folder contains vehicle data from rotating at various rates, with the light held either horizontal or at a 30 deg downward angle.

"rotationRatesPhotodiodes": script to analyze data and determine rotation rates

Data files in order of ending number:
20 dps - horizontal light
40 dps - horizontal light
80 dps - horizontal light
140 dps - horizontal light
220 dps - horizontal light
20 dps ~30deg above horizontal
40 dps ~30deg above horizontal
80 dps ~30deg above horizontal
140 dps ~30deg above horizontal
220 dps ~30deg above horizontal
